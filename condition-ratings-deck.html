The Problem
02 / 09

Our current scale creates friction
at every customer touchpoint

1

Numeric abstraction

What's the real difference between an 8+ and a 9? Customers can't intuit quality from an abstract number — they have to stop and look it up.

2

Scale fragmentation

Sell Used uses a 1–5 scale. The buy side uses 7–10. These systems are completely disconnected — confusing sellers who then become buyers.

3

Hidden condition definitions

Indepth descriptions of conditions do not exist, the sentence that provides more information must be launched in a drawer by clicking a link — not visible at the moment of decision.

4

Expectation misalignment

Survey data shows that buyers who receive used goods, sometimes feel that the quality falls short of the rating — pointing to both evaluation inconsistency and unclear standards.

Proposed New Scale
06 / 09

Five tiers. Plain language. Instant clarity.

Cosmetic ratings only — all items tested and verified fully functional. Developed with evaluator consensus across the Used operations team.

Excellent
Like-new, no visible signs of wear.
Pristine, like new. No visible signs of wear: scuffing or handling marks on any surface or component. Finish is perfect. The finest used condition available. Fully functional with no performance issues.
Very Good
Gently used, minimal signs of wear.
Shows light cosmetic wear. May have minimal scratches, scuffing or rubber imperfections present. Overall gently used condition, clean finish. Fully functional with no performance issues.
Good
Shows moderate wear consistent with regular use.
Shows moderate wear and signs of regular use, including scratches, scuffs, or possible minor brassing. Fully functional with no performance issues.
Fair
Well used with visible cosmetic flaws, scuffs, and marks.
Shows obvious cosmetic wear including significant scuffs, noticeable scratches, and possible brassing. Well used. Fully functional with no performance issues.
Rough
Shows considerable wear, finishing loss, and above-average signs of hard use.
Shows excessive wear, including significant paint and finish loss, exposed metal, deep scratches, and possible dents. Heavily used. Fully functional with no performance issues.
