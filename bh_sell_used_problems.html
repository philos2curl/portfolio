The Meta-Problem
The current flow treats the seller as a logistics problem to be processed, not a customer making a financial decision.
Every stage where the seller is uninformed, uncertain, or surprised is a moment where they abandon, dispute, or don't return.
Surface value early — quote transparency builds commitment before the seller second-guesses the transaction.
Reduce friction at every handoff — especially submission and shipping, where committed sellers abandon.
Communicate proactively — silence after shipping creates anxiety and drives support ticket volume.
Make store credit the obvious choice — it's B&H's strongest conversion lever and it's currently underleveraged.
